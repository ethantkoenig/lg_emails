üë™ Garden City Seminar: Honoring Your Parents

Sun, Jul 14 2019 — 5:30 p.m. – 6:30 p.m.

As part of the Dysfunctional Families series, often the way our culture teaches adults to make peace with hurt from family of origin is to distance one's self from parents and forgive at a distance and with lots of boundaries. This approach may not be as biblical as we think. Boundaries are defined differently in Eastern culture and biblically as well. This seminar will examine how to honor one's parents as an adult and bring an Eastern and Biblical perspective to a difficult topic.

This seminar will be facilitated by our very own Ron and Keren (yay!), plus some other guy named Fred.
